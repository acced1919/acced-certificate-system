About Us

Ahmadiyya Center for Computer Education (ACCEd)

(An ISO Affiliated Centre & Approved Computer Courses Provider)

Ahmadiyya Center for Computer Education (ACCED.) Was started in october 2001 by the kind approval of Hadhrat Mirza Tahir Ahmad Khalifatul Massih IVth under the management of Majlis Khuddamul Ahmadiyya Bharat(An auxiliary organisation of Ahmadiyya Youth.)

Ahmadiyya Center for Computer Education (ACCEd) is a recognized and ISO-affiliated training institute located in Qadian. We are committed to delivering high-quality, certified computer courses to empower students from all backgrounds.

We offer job-oriented training programs for students ranging from 5th grade to post graduate level. Our goal is to provide practical digital skills that open doors to better career opportunities both in India and abroad.
